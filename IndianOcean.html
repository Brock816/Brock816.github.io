The Oceans of Our Planet

Indian Ocean
Sunda Deep is a trench located in the Indian Ocean near Indonesia, and has a depth of 25,344 feet. Every year, the Indian Ocean expands by 20 centimeters, due to the polar ice caps melting. A whopping 40% of the Oil supply in the world comes from the Indian Ocean! It is also known as being the warmest body of water on Earth.

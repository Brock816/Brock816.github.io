The Oceans of Our Planet

Atlantic Ocean
The Atlantic Ocean formed during the Jurassic Period. and is 6.5 times the size of the United States. The Atlantic Ocean is the world's richest fishing area. Please make a donation in an effort to save endangered species living in the Atlantic including the whales, manatee, seals, sea lions, dolphins, albatrosses, auks, petrels, turtles, due to pollution and overfishing.
